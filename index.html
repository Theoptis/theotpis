Introducing the ASI Model: Theoptis’ Specialized Threat Intelligence System

Theoptis is built around a dedicated threat intelligence system known as the ASI model, which is a proprietary form of artificial intelligence developed exclusively by and for Theoptis. Unlike general purpose AI, ASI (Artificial Security Intelligence) is mission specific, focused solely on security, resilience, and protection.

Designed for deployment across secure networks, critical infrastructure, intelligence agencies, and defense environments, ASI delivers real-time cyber defense, intelligent threat detection, and constant adaptive encryption. It is not intended for public use but rather serves as an internal high-grade AI model crafted to meet the advanced security demands of Theoptis and its partners.

ASI represents a new category of intelligent defense—built to think broadly and strategically about one thing only: security.
